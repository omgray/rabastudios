ABOUT

The year is 2017. I Just wrapped up my first DP gig, working for a notable brand at iHeart Media in Riviera Beach Florida.I had no idea what to expect before shooting, but for the first time, the fear I usually had before doing new things felt more like passion instead, hunger. The Company Raba Studios went by the name Nakama Art Co. Nakama's focus was sharing local and international artists' journeys, creative collaborations through the film and music medium.(for example: poetry videos, short stories etc.). While also giving these same artists a platform through themed in person events(AFA charity jamboree, Love Language, iHeart Haiti Charity event). Fast forward to 2021 we've just finished our first Pop-up in Long Beach California at Play Nice LB, working with different creatives in California on musical content and curation showed me the impact of collaborative efforts in the community. Birthing the new and improved RABA Studios. Raba means many different things in other languages(Greatness, God, Money). In the English language it's an abbreviation for my full name. Randy Adrian Blake Accius, Father of 3 multi-disciplined creative in the fields of Film, Music Production and Graphic Art.
